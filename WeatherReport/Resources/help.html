How to use?

1. How do I use?

All you need to do is press + button on top right to add your favourite place as a bookmark.

2. What are the features?

You can show the bookmark list. On swipe left , you can remove your bookmarks.

3. How can I add bookmark any location?

On clicking of + button at top right of screen, you can tap at any location and it will add a pin to map. After pressing save button, your selected location will be add on list
